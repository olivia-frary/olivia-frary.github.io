    Projects

    Here are the projects I am currently working on for the Hjelmen Lab.

    Investigating the Relationship between Heteromorphic Sex Chromosomes and Organism Longevity

    The purpose of this project is to consider the predictors of longevity in organisms across the tree of life. I am particularly interested in whether there is a correlation between the degree of difference in size of heterogametic sex chromosomes and organism lifespan.

    Building a Large-Scale Phylogeny in R

    The purpose of this project is to make a nice pipeline for creating massive phylogenies. I am currently working on a script that makes a supermatrix of the species and genes available for the phylogeny.
